St. Augustine convent college, founded in 1972 by Maria Fernandez, is dedicated to academic excellence and personal growth. With a passion for empowering students and fostering academic excellence, Maria sought to create an institution that would provide a holistic learning environment. Her commitment to inclusive education ensured that students from various backgrounds could access opportunities for personal and professional development. Under her leadership, our college quickly gained recognition for its rigorous academic standards and dedicated faculty. The college began with a modest enrollment and a few key programs, but Maria’s vision and dedication drove its growth and expansion over the years. Today, this college stands as a testament to Maria Fernandez’s founding principles, continuing to provide high-quality education and fostering a supportive community for students to thrive. Our experienced faculty and modern facilities create a supportive learning environment, empowering students to achieve their goals and make a positive impact in the world. And now, we would like to present an overview of our college for your consideration.

About Us

St. Augustine College is committed to providing quality education and fostering a love for learning in students.Our mission is to inspire and empower students to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally, while instilling a strong sense of moral responsibility


Courses Offered

    At St. Agustine college, we offer a diverse range of streams for 11th and 12th grades to cater to students' varied interests and career aspirations. Each stream is designed to provide a strong academic foundation and prepare students for higher education.
  • Science stream: Focused on subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, this stream prepares students for careers in medicine, engineering, and research. Students will engage in practical experiments and critical thinking to understand scientific concepts.
  • Commerce stream: This stream covers subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies, and Economics. It is ideal for students interested in pursuing careers in business, finance, and management. Students will develop analytical and strategic skills essential for the corporate world.
  • Arts stream: Offering subjects such as English, History, Political Science, and Fine Arts. The arts stream encourages creativity and critical analysis. It is perfect for students looking to explore careers in humanities, design, and social sciences.
